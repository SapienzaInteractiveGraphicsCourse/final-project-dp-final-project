CONTROLS:

W: Move forward

S: Move backward

A: Move leftward

D: Move rightward

GAME:

- The object of the game is to be able to finish the maze with the highest possible score.

- The score increases as you move while it decreases as you touch the glowing cubes

- The game has 9 different levels and to win one you have to finish it with a positive score

OPTIONS:

Difficulty:

Easy : The cubes move slowly and do not take away many points (Easy peasy)

Medium : The cubes begin to reach you quickly and start to hurt (Be careful)

Hard : The cubes are very fast and take away a lot of points (You have to run)
